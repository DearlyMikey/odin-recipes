Margherita Pizza Recipe!

Desciption

Pizza is an Italian-origin dish made of a round, flat dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings, then baked until crisp and melty. From classic Margherita to meat-lovers and veggie styles, pizza is endlessly customizable and globally loved for its crispy crust, gooey cheese, and bold flavors.

Ingredients

  • 1 ball pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce (crushed tomatoes with salt and herbs)
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Optional: garlic, grated Parmesan, chili flakes

How to Make!

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C) and place a baking stone or tray inside.
  2. Stretch dough into a round shape on parchment paper or floured surface.
  3. Spread sauce over the dough, leaving a border.
  4. Add cheese and drizzle with olive oil.
  5. Bake for 10–12 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  6. Top with fresh basil before serving.
